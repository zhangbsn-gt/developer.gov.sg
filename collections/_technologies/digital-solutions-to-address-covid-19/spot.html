SPOT - Safe Distancing Robot

A four-legged robot which will be helping to assist the safe distancing efforts at parks, gardens and nature reserves managed by NParks, including Parks Connectors, Pulau Ubin as well as parks managed by town councils.

SPOT, built by Boston Dynamics and the first robotic platform we are developing DOSS software capabilities on. It will help to estimate the number of visitors in the parks and will broadcast a recorded message reminding park visitors to observe safe distancing measures. This also helps to eliminate the headcount for manpower and labour needed for park patrols, helping to minimise physical contact among staff, safe distancing ambassadors and park visitors which will in turn helps to lower their risk of exposure to the COVID-19 virus.

For more information on SPOT and its features, used cases and collaborations, visit https://www.developer.gov.sg/technologies/sensor-platforms-and-internet-of-things/spot.
