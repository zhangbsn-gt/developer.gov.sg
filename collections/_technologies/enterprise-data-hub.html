Overview

The Enterprise Data Hub (EDH) is an inter-agency data-sharing platform that provides public officers with easy access to verified, identifiable business data.

You can use it to verify the legitimacy of a business, learn about a companyâ€™s profile and the government support it has received, and cross-reference agency data with datasets on the EDH for new insights.

Currently, datasets from the Ministry of Manpower, Inland Revenue Authority of Singapore, Accounting and Corporate Regulatory Authority, and Central Provident Fund Board are available.

Get Started

The EDH is accessible on your Government Standard Image Build (GSIB) and GoMax devices at edh.gov.sg. To find out more, contact GovTech-edh@tech.gov.sg.
