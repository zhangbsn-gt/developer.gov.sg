Overview

GovTech has been migrating an increasing number of projects to the Cloud. Operating and managing an environment consisting of multiple Clouds and services pose numerous challenges.

There is also a need to transform operations into 'x'Ops in order to support multiple projects within the GCC.

Join us at this Meetup to gain insights on GovTech's Cloud transformation.
