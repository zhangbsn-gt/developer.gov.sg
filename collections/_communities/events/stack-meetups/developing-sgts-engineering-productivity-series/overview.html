Overview

To launch the year's STACK Meetups, Government Chief Digital Technology Officer (GCDTO), Chan Cheow Hoe and Director in charge of the Singapore Government Tech Stack (SGTS), Kevin Ng will share GovTech's engineering productivity approach.

For this session, we will be joined by Director, Engineering and Engineering Lead (Singapore) of Carousell, Ketan Mujumdar who will talk about the tech marketplace's development practices.

Who should attend: STACK Community, including tech leadership; Levelling: All
