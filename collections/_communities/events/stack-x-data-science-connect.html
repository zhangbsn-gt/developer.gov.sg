Afternoon Tracks (With Live Q&A)

In the afternoon, we have an exciting line-up of speakers across 3 concurrent tracks. These tracks will be led by technical experts on topics such as Data Transformation, Data Products, Data Engineering, AI Applications and Video Analytics.

Each track will run from 2:00 PM - 5 :00 PM and offer attendees the opportunity to interact with speakers through live Q&A sessions. Find out more about each track below.

Track 1: Data Science & Data Transformation

Join this track to learn how Government agencies are tackling challenges when it comes to Data Science and Data Transformation at the organisational level. Speakers will be sharing on some of the best practices they have adopted as transformation enablers.

{% include_relative includes/stack-x-dsc-2022-itinerary.html itinerary=datatransformation %}

Track 2: Data Products & Data Engineering

Join this track if you are interested to know how the Government is partnering with industry to democratise the use of data for public good. Speakers will share on the suite of central platform, data infrastructure and engineering capabilities they have developed in accordance with data governance policies.

{% include_relative includes/stack-x-dsc-2022-itinerary.html itinerary=dataengineering %}

Track 3: AI Applications and Video Analytics Track

Join this track to find out about different AI approaches that private companies and government have adopted, and discover the range of AI applications and solutions available.
